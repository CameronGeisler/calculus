Theorem 6.1.9. Trigonometric derivatives.

\begin{equation*} \boxed{\begin{array}{ll} \dfrac{d}{dx} \sin{x} = \cos{x} \amp \dfrac{d}{dx} \csc{x} = -\csc{x} \cot{x} \\[10pt] \dfrac{d}{dx} \cos{x} = -\sin{x} \amp \dfrac{d}{dx} \sec{x} = \sec{x} \tan{x} \\[10pt] \dfrac{d}{dx} \tan{x} = \sec^2{x} \amp \dfrac{d}{dx} \cot{x} = -\csc^2{x} \end{array}} \end{equation*}
