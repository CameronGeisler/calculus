The derivative of \(f\) is a function whose outputs are the slopes of \(f\text{.}\) Intuitively, the outputs for the derivative of a function come from evaluating the associated limit \(\lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{h}\) for each value of \(x\) in the domain of \(f\text{,}\) and plotting the results.
