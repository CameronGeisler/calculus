Theorem 4.1.2. Positive derivative implies increasing.

Let \(f\) be a function, differentiable on \((a,b)\text{.}\) Then,

  • If \(f'(x) > 0\) for all \(x \in (a,b)\text{,}\) then \(f\) is increasing on \((a,b)\text{.}\)

  • If \(f'(x) \lt 0\) for all \(x \in (a,b)\text{,}\) then \(f\) is decreasing on \((a,b)\text{.}\)

  • If \(f'(x) = 0\) for all \(x \in (a,b)\text{,}\) then \(f\) is constant on \((a,b)\text{.}\)
